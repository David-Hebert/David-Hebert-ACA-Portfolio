David J. Hebert

Born in Leander, I grew up in the late 90's, early 00's surrounded by this new wave of technology and internet. I had a strong interest in tech from an early age, trying to take the family computer apart and figure out how it worked by age ten.

Unfortunately, much of that interest went on the back burner as I got into middle school, and had been practically taken off the stove by the time I hit highschool. The workload of school, as well as multiple sports basically sapped me of most of my free time.

It wasn't until I'd graduated from highschool and been forced into a job as a mechanic, something I enjoyed doing on my own time, but entirely hated having to do it every day, on other people's cars, did I start to seek an out. With a bit of playing around and a lot of googling, I'd built a few simple, static sites.

With the hopes of getting out of the automotive industry, I've started a journey in trying to change my career to web development.
